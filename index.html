Offline

Maps and Navigation

Features

Maps

Create maps and to have them ready even without Internet connection.

Ideal for remote places, exploring nature, or traveling overseas.

GPS

Unleash the power of the GPS on your device.

It is completely independent from the Internet and works any place on planet Earth.

Directions

Add marks to the points of interest in your map, or to the starting and ending locations of your navigation.

They are also completely available offline.

Navigation

Get navigation for walking and driving.

Share

Share your maps with other, simple, fast.

Offload

Save disk on your device and store your maps on the cloud, download them again when needed.

Pricing

  • Free on the App Store
  • No ads
  • No subscriptions
  • No analytics
  • No monetising with your data

Privacy Browser is completely free to use, forever.

Offline Cloud

Offline Cloud is an In-App Purchase, it is a one time only purchase, and it is handled completely by the App Store. By purchasing it you can offload your maps to the Cloud, freeing disks space in your device.

This also allows you to share your maps.
